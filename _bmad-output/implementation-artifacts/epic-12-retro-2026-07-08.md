## 4. What Was Challenging / Growth Areas

**Amelia (Developer):** The genuine subtlety of the epic was the 18-HP winnability crux, and it was NOT a trivial HP swap. All three class kits carry `baseline_hp` 18, and the naive focus-fire `LiveCombatResolver` scripted hero provably DIES at 18 HP on a full live walk — I verified it empirically (throwaway probe, since removed): seed 4242 at 18 HP, warrior sword DEFEAT r5, ranger bow DEFEAT r6, pyromancer staff VICTORY (staff is strong ranged). The root cause (throwaway trace) is that the dominant 18-HP death is a STATIONARY hero eating repeated ash-seer detonations — `marked_tile_detonated` only hits if the hero ENDS a turn on the marked cell — NOT melee. So detonation-dodge (a hard mark-end-cell constraint) is THE survival mechanic that makes all three classes winnable at 18 HP, and the warrior is the winnability bottleneck. That's why AC2 forced a strengthened LoS-aware reference driver, not a threading of 18 into the existing driver (which would reproduce the 11.3 death and fail loud — correct fail-loud behavior, but not a passing winnability proof). It took empirical seed curation to get the approved batch right.

**Charlie (Senior Dev / Reviewer):** 12.2 was the one story with a Med finding, and it's worth being honest about it: the winnability proof is NARROWER than AC2's stated intent. `ReferenceCombatDriver`'s catalog proves 18-HP winnability for `small_combat_basic` / `SIZE_SMALL` boards with NEUTRAL affinity ONLY — but the shipped interactive path (`begin_interactive_combat_node`) also hosts the class loadout on `elite_combat` (`medium_combat_basic` / `SIZE_MEDIUM`) nodes AND applies the node's assigned affinity (Scorched hazard DoT / Darkness / Cursed) before hero placement. AC2's own wording says "Small/**Medium**," and the driver's `resolve(...)` even accepts affinity params the catalog never passes. It's not a correctness defect — the proven family (the depth-0 opening/Small neutral fights) is the primary hands-on entry, and the on-screen human is presumed ≥ the reference driver — but the elite/affinity live surface is UNPROVEN by the harness before 10.4 consumes it. I deferred it as a fast-follow (Med, `[Review][Defer]`): either extend the catalog with a Medium/elite seed × class row + at least one Scorched-affinity live seed, or explicitly scope AC2 to the Small-neutral family and assign the Medium/affinity proof to 10.4/10.6. That's the one substantive thing Epic 12 did NOT fully close.

**Dana (QA Engineer):** Two things needed careful verification because they were the highest-risk seams. First, the hero-defense shield seam introduced the FIRST enemy-phase `combat`-stream draw (`AttackCommand.roll_shield_block` on incoming enemy attacks against a shielded hero) — which meant the enemy-phase simulation copy's stream had to be synced back to the run-level stream.
